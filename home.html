About Me


Oh, didn't see you there.

Hi! As you can see from the header, my name is Shalomeira Valencia Winata and you can simply call me Shalom. I was born and raised in Indonesia, specifically in a city called Surakarta. I am an 18 year old Taurean who loves to read contemporary fiction novels while listening to my Spotify playlist and binge-watch Netflix series and movies. I am currently studying Information System for Business in Ciputra University Surabaya. If you ask me how did I end up here, I would say that it was all fate. And I'll leave it at that :)

I would consider myself as someone who is quiet introverted. I am not the kind of person who can easily initiate a conversation with someone new. But worry not, my introvertness does not limit myself to have an open mind. I tend to... listen to others' perspectives on things before I decide what my thought on that particular topic/issue would be. Due to this, I am also open to experiencing new things. I would say organization is one of my strengths, though I do not keep a journal. I tend to at least plan ahead the day on my head and try to complete it all. When it comes to how I speak, I am pretty straight-forward. I consider this as something positive because this character of mine makes me have the courage to share my views/opinions with others.

"No one is perfect," they say. "True that," I say. Like everybody else, I am still a work in progress. Despite being a second year university student, I am still sometimes struggling with time management. Due to my entertaining hobbies, I tend to procrastinate my work(s) which then leads to hectic final days (of submission) that caused me stress. I am also quite the perfectionist so, I tend to take longer time to make a decision. My perfectionism also results on me being quiet the overthinker because I always consider the 'what-ifs' and the worst possible thing to happen.

When someone asks me, "What do you see yourself doing in the future?" I think I would reply it with a smile and change the subject (subtly) because I have actually been asking myself the same thing over and over again. I am still not sure what exactly is the thing that I want to pursue nor what my passion really is. All I know, is that I want to live happily and comfortably. I do always have a dream of living abroad, though. So to reach that point, I have thought about getting my master's degree in a university abroad (with scholarship) and maybe settle there. Or maybe I would have a decent job in technology (hopefully somewhere abroad) after getting my bachelor's degree. My skill in coding is not that good so, I think I prefer a job in analytics or maybe as a UI/UX designer. I am still struggling a lot with these IS stuff though. So, for the time being, I aim to really focus on school and try to excel at least one of the classes.
